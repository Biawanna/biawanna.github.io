About

As a Senior Unity Developer, Ash excels in various aspects of software development. With a background in computer science and music, Ash has led diverse projects, including 2D/3D game development, multiplayer features with Photon Fusion, and interactive installations. Ash's skills include Unity, C#, XR SDKs, AR, ZED camera integration, AWS, Firebase, JavaScript, HTML, Python, REST API, and WebSocket. Proficient in 3D modeling and animation, Ash constantly pushes technological boundaries to enhance user experiences. Previously, Ash was a recording artist and producer for 7 years. This blend of technical and creative skills defines Ash's unique development approach.
